PDF Text Search & Highlight

Upload a PDF file to search and highlight text within it.

Instructions

  1. Upload your PDF document using the file selector above.
  2. Enter the text you want to find and highlight in the PDF.
  3. Enable OCR if your PDF contains scanned text or images.
  4. Click "Process PDF" and wait for the highlighted version to download.

Important Notes

  • OCR processing may take longer for large documents.
  • OCR highlights might not be as precise as direct text search.
  • The application supports both regular PDFs and scanned documents.
